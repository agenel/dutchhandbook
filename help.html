How it Works

More Dutch is designed to be more than just static rules. Learn how to get the most out of our interactive cheat sheets and practice tools.

1

Explore the Library

Our library contains 15 high-quality Dutch grammar modules. Each sheet is designed to be a complete standalone reference for a specific topic, from simple nouns to complex word order rules.

layers

Progressive Levels

We've categorized everything by CEFR levels to match your current progress:

A1 Beginner A2 Elementary B1 Intermediate
manage_search

Smart Discovery

Use the global search or filter by level to find exactly what you're struggling with. Our search even looks inside the content of the modules!

2

Grammar Cheat Sheets

The core of More Dutch. These aren't just PDF files; they are interactive learning modules built for modern browsers.

visibility_off

Active Recall (Test Mode)

Toggle Test Mode in any sheet to blur the translations. This forces your brain to recall the Dutch word before seeing the answer. Just click a blurred item to reveal it!

stars

Mastery Tracking

Once you've memorized a sheet, mark it as Mastered. This updates your progress bar on the home page and lets you hide mastered content in the library.

3

Practice Tools

Take your knowledge beyond theory. Our tools are designed for high-speed repetition to build intuition.

auto_awesome

Rapid-Fire Drills

Practice noun genders (de/het), verb conjugations, and word order using our specialized training tools.

history_edu

Sentence Building

Master the tricky Dutch word order by assembling sentences block-by-block. We'll give you instant feedback on your placement.

4
