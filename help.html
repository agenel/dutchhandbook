How it Works

More Dutch is designed to be more than just static rules. Learn how to get the most out of our interactive cheat sheets and practice tools.

1

Explore the Library

Our library contains 15 essential Dutch grammar modules ranging from A1 to B1. Each sheet is carefully designed to be concise, visual, and focused on practical usage.

filter_list

Filter by Level

Quickly find modules for your current level (A1, A2, or B1).

search

Instant Search

Looking for "Negation" or "Inversion"? Just start typing.

2

Interactive Sheets

Every grammar sheet has two modes: Study Mode and Test Mode. Use Study Mode to review rules, then toggle Test Mode to hide answers and challenge yourself.

visibility_off

Test Mode

Blurs translations and conjugations. Click to reveal when you're ready.

verified

Track Mastery

Mark a module as "Mastered" once you're confident. We'll save your progress locally.

3

Practice Tools

Grammar is best learned through repetition. Our interactive tools take the rules from the cheat sheets and turn them into rapid-fire exercises.

bolt

Rapid Drills

Practice noun genders, verb forms, and sentence building.

link

Integrated Learning

Quizzes are linked directly to the grammar modules they test.
